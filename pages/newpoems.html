Well, I figured since we don't/won't get much time together, that this could be a place for our memories, for our feelings, a place to grow. of truth and honesty. Where we can express or rather, I can express my artistic emotions that sometimes don't get displayed in a concrete way that you can view and be reminded just how much you mean to me.

Closer

The time has come,
Let's take things slow,
Endless spiral sinking,
I'm trapped in your love.

To the hallow depths we go,
Each moment I hold without blinking,
The patience, Together closer as one.

Sparks

It's special we share,
More than just a sun,
A passion fruit, symbols of love,
A spark from within, rekindling fire.

With grace and beauty,
You soar to new heights,
Encompasses by you, sprinting to catch up,
To grab your hand and walk side by side,

Leaving behing sins of our past,
Reborn anew man i strive for your love,
Truthful and honest, I'll remain until the end,
I'm done playing games, you can feel this new change,

No Matter where you go, or who you see,
No matter the time, the weather, the reason,
Closing my eye's, your all I see.

Pewter

I know you don't think I'm ready,
Tough, sincere, or emotionally steady,
But when I said I want you,
genuinely I meant I would see it by through.
You ask me to date, to see another,
It's true I listened but I want this mother,
Just let us explore and cuddle under the stars,
It's a life I want more than the fancy cars.
You make me happy, sleeping on the couch,
Going to the beach, I was always a grouch.
You can't deny we finish each other,
I Will give you space to with another,
No longer jealous or afraid of the future,
We could flash like shiny pewter.
It does not mean we have to fuck,
or suck and quack like loving ducks,
Times will be tough, I'm not asking for much,
you would be the last, I want you forever and such.
Now it's even tough your trapped inbetween,
Me and you two different at the seam,
Never felt so grounded for myself,
Lifting me higher in helping yourself.
The rhymes I find guidance,
I believe the experience will enlighten,
The time, never a nuisance,
Left in the dark, the light will brighten,
Our future, your future, my future, the son.

Searching

Rain, drains down the love of tomorrows,

Never knowing the sorrows coming,

Washing, cleansing follows, beginning again,

Puddles pooling, drowning hollows, seeing an-end not-near,

Through thick humid haze, swallows meaning, prented it's-clear blindly,

Wallows, freeing an-end four-years slightly foreseeing,

Cold air, warm breeze, whatever comes will be ours to dream.

Fish

Fish swim with the currents.
Shifting with the changes.
Stuck in between, pulling me down.
One current is warm, bright.
Another is cold, devoid of light.

Seeing both sides. I chose the right.
For the past haunts my choices.
I can't erase the past but I can write the future.

Right is the warm light.
But sometimes it shines too bright.
Enough to burn my skin and leave me scared.
The sins of my past leave noticeable scars.

Be(a)st

The warm breeze of mid summer's night,
calm air with a bright moonlight.
Everything so clear, with the ocean near,
The salt water filled from their tears.
Aware, the winds change into a beast,
Right is west. Stuck in between. Left is east.
New beginnings never felt so old.
Especially when it starts off cold.
No where to go, staring into the light,
I have to know, is this my fear?
Courage, at it's very least.
Tame the beast!
New beginnings.

Simmer

Ever since March 26th 2017, a pot has been stirring inside.
I simmer and simmer, only to overheat and boil over the lid.
My contents spill out, I simmer some more, but my patience is boiling.
Not sure what to make, but the water is ready, I must not be late.

I don't know how I have gotten this way, I'm obbsesing over a date.
It's a metaphor, for forever is tomorrow or this could be beginning.
Spinning down a twisted spiral, closer to reach the ending bid.
Of how much your love will cost, to walk side by side, hand in hand.
